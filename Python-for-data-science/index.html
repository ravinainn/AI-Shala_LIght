Description

Welcome to our comprehensive Python for Data Science program! This program is designed to empower students with essential knowledge and skills in machine learning techniques. Here's what you can expect:

  • Program Duration: The program spans over 4 weeks, with 3 lectures per week, ensuring an interactive and engaging learning experience.
  • Key Topics Covered: Participants will learn various machine learning techniques, including training models, evaluating performance, and exploring different algorithms to construct effective learning models.
  • Practical Application: Our program emphasizes problem-solving through the practical application of machine learning and artificial intelligence techniques
  • Interview Guidance: Participants will receive valuable guidance on machine learning interview topics, and they will have the opportunity to observe sample interviews to gain insights into the interview process.
  • Assignments and Projects: Participants will be required to complete a minimum of three assignments. Exceptional performance may lead to a 3-month extension to work on an AI-based project, providing an opportunity to showcase their skills.
  • Certification: Upon successful completion of the program, participants will receive an eCertificate, recognizing their accomplishment and validating their newly acquired machine learning skills.
